Library Facilities

Book Lending Services

  • Academic textbooks and reference books
  • General knowledge and competitive exammaterials
  • Novels and non-fiction for leisure reading
  • Limited borrowing (with student ID ormembership)

Study Area

  • Quiet and peaceful reading rooms
  • Comfortable seating arrangements
  • Individual study desks or cubicles
  • Proper lighting and ventilation

Internet and Technology Access

  • Free or paid Wi-Fi for students
  • Computers for internet research or assignments
  • Charging ports or power outlets at desks

Clean and Safe Environment

  • Clean washrooms
  • Drinking water facility
  • Air-conditioning (in some libraries)
  • CCTV surveillance or security staff
  • Group discussion rooms

Printing and Photocopying

  • Basic print, scan, and photocopy services
  • Usually available for a small fee
  • Support for printing assignments, notes, or articles

Flexible Timings

  • Open during student-friendly hours

Reference Materials

  • Educational magazines and current affairs journals
  • CSample papers and previous exam questions

Assistance and Guidance

  • On-site librarian or staff to help find books
  • Basic research help or catalog navigation
  • Study guidance (in some student-focused libraries)
